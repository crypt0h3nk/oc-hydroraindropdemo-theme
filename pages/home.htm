Demo

This is the Hydro Raindrop demo theme that will show you how to integrate Hydro Raindrop to your OctoberCMS powered website.

Requirements

  • The HydroCommunity.Raindrop plugin requires Rainlab.User plugin to be installed.
  • PHP 7.1
  • The Hydro iOS / Android app
